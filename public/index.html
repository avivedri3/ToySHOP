Documentation - Toys Api

The types of routes you can do:

  • To display all documents of toys

    (GET) ⇨ https://toyshop2021.herokuapp.com/toys

    You can also switch between pages ⇨ https://toyshop2021.herokuapp.com/toys/?page=0/1

  • To search a toys with name/info

    (GET) ⇨ https://toyshop2021.herokuapp.com/toys/search

  • To get the amount of toys in categoery:

    (GET) ⇨ https://toyshop2021.herokuapp.com/toys/cat

  • To add toys

    (POST) ⇨ https://toyshop2021.herokuapp.com/toys

  • To edit toys

    (PUT) ⇨https://toyshop2021.herokuapp.com/toys/edit

  • To delete toys

    (DELETE) ⇨ https://toyshop2021.herokuapp.com/toys/del

  • Find a toy between minimum and maximum price

    (GET) ⇨https://toyshop2021.herokuapp.com/toys/price/?min=X&max=X
